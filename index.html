Bone Editor - Ultimate Skeleton Creation Tool for Unity

Revolutionize Your 3D Character and Object Rigging with Bone Editor!

Are you tired of spending hours manually rigging skeletons for your 3D models? Do you want a powerful, yet easy-to-use tool that simplifies the process of creating and editing skeletons in Unity? Look no further!

Bone Editor is here to transform your workflow, allowing you to create humanoid rigs, complex skeletons, and more with just a few clicks.

Whether you're working on characters, vehicles, creatures, or mechanical objects, Bone Editor provides the flexibility and automation you need to bring your models to life. With features like automatic vertex binding, bone visualization, and JSON save/load functionality, Bone Editor is the ultimate solution for both beginners and experienced developers.
